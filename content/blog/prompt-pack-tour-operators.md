Ten prompts that replace ten hours of your week. Below is the short version — the full pack with context, example inputs, and expected outputs is in the download link at the bottom.

## What's in the pack

1. **Proposal first-draft generator** — turns an inquiry form into a full proposal in your voice
2. **Guide day-briefing writer** — compiles the guide's day sheet from vendor confirms + guest profiles
3. **Supplier outreach email** — professional, warm, and tone-matched to destination
4. **Incident report summarizer** — turns a guide's text notes into a clean, filable report
5. **Guest welcome letter** — personalized to the guest's travel history and preferences
6. **Itinerary day-at-a-glance** — compresses a long itinerary into a guest-readable summary
7. **Vendor capability matrix** — compares 3 suppliers across the criteria that matter
8. **Trip accounting categorizer** — sorts receipt data into your P&L categories
9. **Post-trip recap** — for guests, for ops notes, or both
10. **FAQ generator** — builds destination-specific FAQ blocks from your past client questions

## How to use them

All prompts follow the same shape:
- **System context**: your brand voice + constraints
- **Variables**: {{GUEST_NAME}}, {{DESTINATION}}, {{DATES}}, etc.
- **Task**: what to produce
- **Format**: exactly how to structure the output

Paste the prompt into Claude, fill the variables, and iterate on the output. After 3–4 passes on a given task the prompt becomes genuinely useful. Don't skip the iteration.

## What's not in the pack

Tools that need real-time data from your systems (your vendor database, your live booking platform, your Airtable). Those require a custom build, not a prompt. Book a discovery call if that's what you need.
